Values

These are a few ideologies I value deeply. If you agree with them, we will likely work well together. ü§ù

Be Grateful:

We are surrounded by brilliant people who bring amazing things into our projects and personal lives. Let's identify each other's strengths and hard work. Don't let exemplary work go unnoticed!

Release Frequently, Listen Closely:

We build products for the customer; therefore, we must listen closely to their feedback. It's better to release features fast and adapt to customer feedback than to guess what the customer wants.

Learn From Your Failures:

We are all human; we make mistakes. With every catastrophic failure comes tremendous learning. Allow your mistakes to improve you and your product, then move on.

Be Holistic & Detail Oriented:

Although writing robust, scalable software is often integral to a product's development, don't limit your attention to writing code. Understanding the company's mission and product direction may be just as important.
